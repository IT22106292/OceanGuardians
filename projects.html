Projects

What We Did?

1. Project Blue Haven – A mission to protect endangered sea turtles by conserving their nesting sites across the Caribbean beaches.
2. Project Reef Revival – Dedicated to restoring coral reef ecosystems in the Great Barrier Reef and promoting sustainable tourism practices.
3. Project Whale Sentinel – Monitoring and safeguarding migratory whale populations in the Atlantic Ocean from ship collisions and illegal hunting.
